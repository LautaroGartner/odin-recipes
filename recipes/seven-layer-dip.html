7-Layer Dip

7-layer dip is a big hit at all small gatherings! Refried beans are layered with guacamole, taco-seasoned sour cream, veggies, and cheese; it's perfect for dipping tortilla chips. The dip traditionally takes less time to disappear into bellies than it does to prepare.

Prep time should be around 20 minutes and give you 64 servings for a yield of 1 (9x13-inch) dish

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados. peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 1/2 fresh lime juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 8 ounce sour cream
  • 1 ounce taco seasoning mix
  • 16 ounce refried beans
  • 4 Roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bunch green onions, finely chopped
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican-style cheese blend
  • 2 ounce black olives, drained and finely chopped

Directions

  1. Step 1. Mash avocados in a medium bowl to desired consistency. Mix in cilantro, salsa, lime juice, garlic salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Step 2. Blend sour cream and taco seasoning in a small bowl until combined. Set aside.
  3. Step 3. Spread refried beans in a 9x13-inch dish or on a large serving platter. Top with sour cream mixture; cover with guacamole. Top with tomatoes, green onions, Mexican-style cheese blend, and black olives.

Nutrition Facts per serving

42 Calories, 3g Fat, 3g Carbs, 2g Protein.
